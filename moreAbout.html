Alyssa as a Web Developer

Being in the IT field can be overwhelming, especially if you're surrounded by people who has a better understanding on how things are done. Like a small goldfish in the middle of a thriving coral reef filled with glamorous fishes of different kinds. That is how I summarize my first few years in this course. Not a really great start if I may say so myself. But I guess that is just how a normal person would feel whenever they're stepping on a land that they are not familiar with.

Overwhelmingly scary yet fun. I guess that is how I could describe my experience with Web Development. To be honest, I haven't really understood how to program something until recently, during my thesis subject where I was forced to study how things should work. Having to blindly navigate thru stackoverflow to find the answer for a simple question, it was really hard. But once the codes start to run and havning the sense of achievement when it ran successfully, I really enjoyed that feeling. I still couldn't believe I enjoyed those sleepless nights just cause a simple function ran successfully.

It was hard, stressful and tear jerking but I enjoyed it. Just that thought made me want to continue on persuing this line of work.
